Show rows of sliding wooden keys entering the keybox, each carrying small adjustable wooden tangents that stop the melody strings, with a separate protected path of fine sympathetic strings visible beneath or through the keybox. Warm maple and spruce tones, satin varnish, subtle tool-made precision, removable service panels hinted at by clean seams, no labels or logos. Clean neutral studio background, soft directional studio lighting, 3/4 product-photography angle, approximately 50mm lens, high detail, photorealistic.

Natural window light, honest prototype-review atmosphere, no fake brand marks, high detail, realistic wood grain and string reflections.

## Detail Prompt

Photorealistic macro close-up of the nyckelharpa keybox mechanism: a sliding wooden key moving through a guide slot, a replaceable tangent face touching a melody string cleanly, fine sympathetic strings passing safely below with visible clearance, and a polished bridge edge in the background. Shallow depth of field, soft raking light, crisp wood fibers, metal string highlights, high detail, realistic scale cues from fingertips and small setup shims.

## Negative / Avoid

Avoid text, watermarks, logos, impossible string paths, decorative-only keys, extra limbs, distorted hands, fantasy ornament, and mechanisms that block bow access or trap the sympathetic strings.
